About the Weather App

Welcome to the Weather App, your go-to platform for checking the current weather in any city or using your current location to get accurate weather information! The app is designed to provide up-to-date and reliable weather data using real-time sources.

Features:

  • Check weather by city name or use your current location.
  • Get detailed weather information, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and pressure.
  • View sunrise and sunset times for your location.
  • Weather icons to visually represent current conditions.
  • Easy-to-use and user-friendly interface.
